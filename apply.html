Summer 2026 Fellowship

Apply to the Atlas Fellowship

A six-week mentored research program on global education access. You will work with a mentor to research a specific question about who gets access to education and why, and turn it into a literature review written to the standard of a peer-reviewed journal, with a path to submission for publication.

No prior research experience is required. We care about how you think, not what you have already done.

Duration 6 weeks Starts Jul 27 Format Remote Deadline Jul 24

Name an education-access problem you have seen in your own community or country, and explain what you would want to understand about it.
About 300 words. Write in your own voice. There is no right answer and no expected vocabulary. We want to see how you notice a problem and how you think about it.
