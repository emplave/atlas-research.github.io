Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Atlas Research Institute is a youth-led research organization. We take the privacy of our applicants, fellows, and subscribers seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your email address, and, if you choose to answer, how you heard about Atlas.

When you apply to the Atlas Fellowship, we collect your name, email address, school, city, and country, your grade level, and your responses to the application questions.

How we use your data

  • Reviewing and processing fellowship applications
  • Telling you when applications open, and communicating with fellows about deadlines and program updates
  • Understanding which channels bring people to Atlas, from the optional "how did you hear about us" question, so we know where to focus
  • Publishing student-authored work, with the author's consent, in the Atlas Journal of Education Policy and related outputs
  • Improving the program

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes
  • We do not publish personal data without the author's consent
  • We do not use your data for any purpose other than those listed above

Who can access your data

Your personal data is accessible only to Atlas Research Institute leadership and designated program coordinators. We use Google Workspace (Google Forms and Google Sheets) to collect and store it.

Data retention

We retain application and waitlist data for the duration of your involvement with Atlas and for up to 12 months after, unless you ask us to delete it earlier.

Applicants under 18

Atlas primarily serves high school students. If you are under 18, we encourage you to tell a parent or guardian about your participation. Atlas does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have, we will delete it promptly.

Your rights

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw from the program at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, contact us at admin@atlas-research.org.
